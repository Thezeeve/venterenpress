# Demo Script

Use this script when presenting Global Press Network to investors, partners, or clients.

## 1. Open with the product story

- Explain that the platform is a full newsroom operating system, not a single CMS page.
- Emphasize publishing, workflow, subscriptions, multimedia, AI newsroom tools, and global distribution.

## 2. Show the homepage

- Start with breaking news, trending stories, premium content, and regional editions.
- Call out that the homepage can be rebranded and restyled from the admin settings panel.

## 3. Show the article experience

- Open a premium article.
- Highlight the premium lock, correction notices, author card, related stories, comments, and newsletter CTA.
- Mention that article metadata, versions, and workflow states are all persisted in Prisma.

## 4. Show newsroom operations

- Open the editor dashboard.
- Walk through the review queue, scheduled publish calendar, fact-check workflow, and editorial notes.
- Show the journalist workspace and the AI newsroom workbench.

## 5. Show monetization

- Open the pricing page and subscriber account surface.
- Mention metering, billing abstractions, invoices, advertiser inquiry flows, and sponsored content handling.

## 6. Close with scale and trust

- Open the operations and status pages.
- Emphasize Redis queues, S3-compatible media, audit logs, rate limiting, and launch checklist documentation.
- End by noting the platform is ready to be renamed to the final brand without structural rework.
